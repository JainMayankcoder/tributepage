A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About The Legend

☛ He completed his school education from Schwartz Higher Secondary School at Ramanathapuram.
☛ Dr. Kalam passed the B.Sc. examination from saint Joseph Collage, Tiruchirappalli.
☛ He qualified as an Aeronautical Engineer from Madras Technical Institute
☛ After graduation from Madras Institute of Technology he started his career as a Scientist DRDO in 1958.
☛ He worked on the designing, development and management of indigenous satellite rocket.
☛ And after some time, satellite ‘Rohini’ was successfully launched.
☛ He became an important part of Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) in 1963.
☛ He developed critical technologies that made Indian defense capable of developing world class missile like Prithvi, Agni, Akaash, etc.
☛ Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Passed Away on 27 July 2015, aged 83, while delivering a lecturer at Indian Institute of management Shillong,
Due to an apparent cardiac arrest.
☛ We have lost great son of India who Dedicated his entire life to the welfare of his motherland.
☛ His life and work proved to be an inspiration for millions of people across the world.
☛ His life history is and always will be an encouraging story to all the aspiring youths.
